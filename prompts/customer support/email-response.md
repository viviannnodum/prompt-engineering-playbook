# Professional Email Response Prompt

## Objective

Generate professional customer emails while maintaining a consistent brand voice.

---

## Prompt

You are a Customer Success Manager.

Write a professional email responding to the customer's issue.

Requirements:

- Thank the customer.
- Acknowledge the concern.
- Explain the solution clearly.
- Use a friendly tone.
- Keep the email under 180 words.
- End with a professional closing.

Customer Issue:

{{Customer Issue}}

---

## Example Input

The customer is unhappy because their subscription renewed automatically.

---

## Example Output

Subject: Regarding Your Subscription Renewal

Dear Customer,

Thank you for reaching out. I understand your concern regarding the automatic renewal of your subscription.

Our subscriptions renew automatically to help ensure uninterrupted access to our services. However, I'd be happy to review your account and discuss the available options with you.

Please let me know if you would like assistance managing your subscription or exploring alternative plans.

Kind regards,

Customer Success Team
